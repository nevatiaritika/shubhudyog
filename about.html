About Us

Constituted in the year 1987, Shubh Udyog is an intensely expanding electrical contracting organization engaged in Electrical engineering and installation works, providing quality work in commercial, industrial and residential markets.

Our committed team of industry professionals bring proficiency, acquaintance and practical know-how to any electrical and renewable energy project regardless of size.
We have carried out the various types of jobs, which include Supply and Erection of H.T. Substraction, Switchgear, Transformers Power Cabling, Street Lighting, Power and lighting Wiring, Computer Data Cabling, Earthing Conductor, Lighting Protection Systems, Seurity Survellience System & P.A. System etc for reputed firms in India.
To carry out the projects to complete contentment of clients, consultants, PMCs and architects we are equipped with skilled engineers and professionals. With every passing year we have become stronger and built sturdy name for our proficiency, our exceptional manpower, apparatus and a structure that permits us take on the challenges. Today Shubh Udyog is professionally recognized customer service organization.
We strive to give our customers complete solution under one roof. As one of the leading Electrical Contractors, we have fostered the development of a quality service culture as our point of differentiation from others.
Shubh Udyog's success has largely been due to its string integration expertise, its commitment to support its customers and ability to absorb and deploy technology. Today Shubh Udyog offers high quality Electrical Implementation.
Shubh Udyog operates from its Office in Mumbai. Office is staffed with trained & experienced engineers and technicians and also equipped with required tools & tackles, equipment and measuring instruments needed to carry any kind of electrical installation work.
