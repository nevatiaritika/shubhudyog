HT SUBSTATION

We offer complete substation construction and installation service for HT Breaker, HT Panel, Transformer, Metering units, Unitised Substations etc. Our engineering with our wide experience meet each electrical and mechanical specification. Our execution staff has the extensive technical information and field experience to install substations in accordance to general rules.
We offer following services:
- Up to 66 kV Substations
- Site and equipment selection
- Transformer installation
- Switchgear Installation
- Testing and commissioning
- Drawing in AutoCAD
- Assistance in obtaining approvals from electrical inspectorate

LT ELECTRICAL EXECUTIONS

We offer complete solution for the requirements of Internal Electrification and LT INstallations. We have done every type and class of installations and are leading the way in meeting client's requirements. Our project engineers and execution teams have wide and practical experience in installation methods and practices.
We offer following services:
- Power Distribution Systems: Supply, Installation and commissioning of LT panels, distribution boards, internal/external cabling, point wiring, earthing conductor, power outlets, indoor and outdoor lighting fixtures etc
- CCTV System: Supply, installation, testing and commissioning of color/B&W close circuit cameras, digital video recorders, external storage devices etc
- Access Control System: Supply, Installation, testing and commissioning of standalone/LAN connectivity access panels, readers, biometric access control system, online attendance software etc
- Fire Detection System: Supply, installation, testing & commissioning of conventional/addressable type fire detection system, fire acknowledgement/repeater panel, FM200 type system etc
- Public addressable System: Supply, Installation, testing & commissioning of audio system, amplifier, speaker, music system, microphones etc
