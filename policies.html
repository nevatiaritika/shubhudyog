Safety

We intend to lay down the utmost stadards of Safety management at Shubh Udyog. Our complete security strategy builds our conventional excellent practice by intensifying existing organizational planning and further implanting the significance of security across all areas of our business. Shubh Udyog has a firm dedication to the safety and well being of each of its workers.
We teach our workers to make sure adherence to not only the pertinent satutory obligations, codes of practices and industry standards but to also take on a risk management approach and apply standards that echo our values. We make certain that safety for personnel, client and property is the first precedence.
A sequence of initiatives have been undertaken in this field. The scope of coverage of these initiatives encompasses not only our employees but also associates, vendor partners and their employees. These include:
- Conception and affirmation to various processes for safety.
- Development of a detailed health, safety and environment (HSE) manual to be followed at all sites, including mishap reporting and investigation.
- In house training and education at all construction sites and at office

QUALITY POLICY

Shubh Udyog is committed to give a service at quality that will meet out client's expectations whilst providing efficient and timely solutions.

The purpose of our Quality is to ensure that all products and services supplied to our clients consistently meet all or exceed their expectations. The company operates a system that regularly evaluates its processes and customer needs, and has plans and goals which will be improved upon year after year.
The policy of Quality is in place to ensure that the best possible products and services are supplied to our clients, and that we are able to meet their requirements as effectively and efficiently as possible.
